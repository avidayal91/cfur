OUR MISSION

The International Day for the Abolition of Slavery is on the 2nd of December.
Human trafficking is at an all-time high. There are more slaves now than in any other time in history. 4.5 million people are subjected to forced sexual exploitation. Most of these are abducted or lured into it as minors. Little girls and children are abused in the most inhumane way, and it has been ignored for way too long.

This affects us personally, and we were deeply shocked and disturbed at how widespread human trafficking is. God has inspired us to act, and we believe that He is working with us and urging us on in this endeavour.

To aware people about this, we will run 65 Kms from Patiala to Chandigarh on 2nd December 2017, we will be accompanied by cyclist for our support. Every amount we raise from this campaign, will go directly to Purnata, who are actively involved in anti-trafficking activities from last 3 years.
